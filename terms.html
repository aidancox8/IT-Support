Terms and Conditions

Last updated: March 2026. These terms apply to all services provided by Tech Neighbour BC.

1. Services

We provide residential IT support services as listed in our service menu. Services are performed in-person at your location or remotely via screen sharing, depending on the nature of the issue and mutual agreement.

We reserve the right to assess any request and determine whether it falls within our scope of services. Out-of-scope requests may be quoted on a case-by-case basis.

2. No Fix, No Fee

We operate on a no fix, no fee basis for the following service types:

  • Virus and malware removal
  • General diagnostics and troubleshooting
  • Account recovery

No fix, no fee does not apply in the following situations:

  • Hardware failure or physical damage beyond repair
  • Issues caused by third-party software or services outside our control
  • Devices that are end-of-life or incompatible with the requested solution
  • Cases where the client declines a recommended course of action that would resolve the issue

If we determine mid-visit that a fix is not possible, we will inform you immediately and no charge will be applied for that visit.

3. Pricing and Payment

  • A minimum call-out fee of $50 CAD applies to all in-person visits, covering the first 30 minutes of service.
  • Additional time is billed at $65 CAD/hr, in 30-minute increments.
  • Remote support is billed at a minimum of $30 CAD for the first 30 minutes, then $50 CAD/hr after.
  • Flat-rate services are priced as listed in our service menu and quoted before work begins.
  • Payment is due at the time of service completion via Interac e-Transfer or PayPal.
  • A travel fee may apply for visits in distant areas of the Lower Mainland.

4. Cancellations and No-Shows

Please provide at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule a scheduled visit.

No-shows or cancellations with less than 2 hours notice may be subject to a $25 CAD cancellation fee.

5. Liability

We take reasonable care when handling your devices and data.

We are not liable for pre-existing hardware issues, data loss, or damage unrelated to our work.

We strongly recommend backing up your data before any service visit. If you do not have a backup, we will inform you of this risk before proceeding.

Our total liability in any situation is limited to the amount paid for the specific service in question.

6. Privacy and Data

We may need to access your device, files, or accounts to perform the requested service.

We will not access, copy, store, or share any personal data beyond what is necessary to complete the service.

We do not retain any login credentials or personal information after a service visit is complete.

7. Remote Support

Remote support sessions require you to download and run a screen-sharing application (such as AnyDesk or similar).

You may end the remote session at any time.

We will not access your device outside of scheduled and agreed-upon sessions.

8. Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to refuse or discontinue service at our discretion, including situations involving unsafe conditions, abusive conduct, or requests that are illegal or outside our capabilities.

9. Changes to These Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
